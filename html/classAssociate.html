Detailed Description

The Associate Class.

An Association consists in a group of associates. Although each associate has a name, they are distinguished by their unique ID. Besides this, each one also works for an Institution and has some scientific areas of interest. They also have access to a Network where the associates can send and receive emails, taking into account the frequency the associates pay the annual pay.
